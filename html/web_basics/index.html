Algarve: Portugal and Its Famous Beaches

Portual is a small country, but it does some things really well:

  • fish
  • wine
  • pastel de nata (or egg tarts)
  • relaxation

There are many great beaches for relaxation, especially in the south of Portugal, in Algarve. Here is a list in the order of my personal perference:

  1. Praia do Telheiro (Shed Beach)
  2. Praia do Beliche (Beliche Beach)
  3. Praia dos Tres Irmaos (Three Brothers Beach)
  4. Praia da Batata (Potato Beach)
